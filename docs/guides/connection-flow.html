    Connection flow

    Guide Flow

    This page explains the path from opening a phone experience to seeing it on TV with the least possible complexity.

    1. Local network

    Keep phone and TV viewer app on the same Wi-Fi. This is the baseline requirement for fast pairing and stable streaming.

    2. Pairing

    Start pairing from the phone and confirm the target TV through QR or code-based flow.

    3. Streaming

    Once paired, the phone drives the experience and sends output to TV in real time while input remains on the phone.
