    Design considerations

    Use this checklist to shape experiences that feel clear on TV and responsive on the phone.

    1. Controls should stay on phone

    Keep interaction detail on phone so the TV remains clean and shared for everyone in the room.

    2. Prioritize big-screen readability

    Use strong contrast, short labels, and clear visual hierarchy so information is readable from couch distance.

    3. Design for shared moments

    When multiple players join, keep TV state synchronized and avoid private-only information on the shared screen.
